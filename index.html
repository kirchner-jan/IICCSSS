The International Interdisciplinary Computational Cognitive Science Summer School (IICCSSS) is an annual educational meeting for students and young researchers interested in computational approaches to brain and cognitive sciences.

In 2022, we will have an IICCSSS one-week event from the 5th to the 9th of September in a hybrid format in Tuebingen, Germany.

We will announce the speakers and schedule soon. Registration will open in early June.

To see what the event will be like, visit our page on the event in 2019.

Organisation of the event is supported by the Computational Cognitive Science Colloquium e.V.
